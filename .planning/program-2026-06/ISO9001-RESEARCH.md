# ISO 9001:2015 × CK工程 — Research, Feature Mapping & Gap Analysis

**Date:** 2026-06-11
**Scope:** How the app helps a HK construction company (a) obtain/retain ISO 9001:2015 certification — which HK Government works lists require — and (b) score better in works-tender technical assessments by producing audit-grade quality records.
**Method:** Online research (all sources cited inline with URLs) + grounding in the actual schema/code (cited as `file:line`).

---

## 0. Executive summary

- ISO 9001 (QMS) certification from an HKAS-accredited certification body is a **standing requirement for contractors on the DEVB List of Approved Contractors for Public Works and the List of Approved Suppliers of Materials and Specialist Contractors** (per WBTC No. 13/2001 and the Contractor Management Handbook), and the Housing Authority has required ISO 9000-series certification of its new-works building contractors **since April 1993**.
- The app is **not** a certification — but it is exactly the kind of *operational evidence engine* a certification audit and a tender technical assessment look for. Its strongest clauses today: **7.5.3 (control of documented information)** via drawing versioning + immutable storage, **8.5.1/8.5.6 (controlled execution and control of changes)** via SI/VO/PTW with frozen approval chains and post-lock immutability, and **9.1 (monitoring & measurement)** via progress history/snapshots and daily logs.
- Biggest gaps vs. ISO 9001: **no NCR/corrective-action workflow with root cause + effectiveness check (10.2)**, **no inspection & test plan (ITP) / hold-point records (8.5.1 verification)**, **no controlled-document register for non-drawing documents (7.5)**, **no internal-audit (9.2) or management-review (9.3) support**, **no calibration (7.1.5) or competence (7.2) records**. Ranked build list in §3.
- HK direction of travel is on the app's side: DEVB TC(W) No. 8/2025 mandates a **Digital Works Supervision System (DWSS)** — digital RISC forms, site diary, inspection records — for capital works contracts > $30M tendered on/after 1 Apr 2026. The app is effectively a subcontractor-scale DWSS; that is a strong bid narrative.

## 1. The ISO 9001:2015 clauses that matter for a site-management app

ISO 9001:2015 merged "documents" and "records" into **documented information**: *maintained* information (procedures, plans, specs — kept current) and *retained* information (records — kept as evidence). ([ISMS.online 7.5](https://www.isms.online/iso-9001/clause-7-5-documented-information/), [iso9001help 7.5](https://iso9001help.co.uk/7.5-Documented-Information.html))

| Clause | What it demands (condensed) | What an auditor samples on site |
|---|---|---|
| **7.5.1–7.5.2 Documented information — general / creating & updating** | The org decides what documented info the QMS needs; each item must be identified (title, date, author, reference), in a suitable format, and **reviewed and approved for suitability** before use. ([Core Solutions 7.5](https://www.thecoresolution.com/clause-7-5-iso-90012015-explained)) | Doc IDs, revision labels, who approved a document and when |
| **7.5.3 Control of documented information** | Right version **available at point of use**; protected from loss/unauthorised change; **distribution, access, retrieval, retention and disposition controlled**; **obsolete documents prevented from unintended use**; changes/revision status identified. ([ecesis 7.5](https://www.ecesis.net/ISO-9001/Documented-Information.aspx)) | Drawing register; is the foreman building from the current revision? Are superseded drawings marked? Can records be altered after the fact? |
| **8.1 Operational planning & control** | Plan processes to deliver the product: criteria for processes & acceptance of products, resources needed, and **documented info retained to have confidence processes were carried out as planned and to demonstrate conformity**. Control of **planned and unintended changes**. ([David Barker 8.1](https://davidbarker.consulting/iso9001/clause-8-1-operational-planning-and-control/), [ISMS.online 8.1](https://www.isms.online/iso-9001/clause-8-1-operational-planning-and-control/)) | Programme/quality plan per project; evidence work followed it; how scope changes were reviewed |
| **8.5.1 Control of production & service provision** | Execute under **controlled conditions**: availability of specs/drawings, monitoring & measurement at appropriate stages, suitable infrastructure, **competent persons**, defined criteria for work release. ([Pretesh Biswas 8.5.1](https://preteshbiswas.com/2023/09/06/iso-90012015-clause-8-5-1-control-of-production-and-service-provision/), [ecesis 8.5–8.7](https://www.ecesis.net/ISO-9001/Production-and-Service-Provision.aspx)) | Method statements, permits for high-risk work, inspection records at stages, who signed off what |
| **8.5.6 Control of changes** | Review and control changes to production "to the extent necessary to ensure continuing conformity", **retaining documented info on the results of the review, the person(s) authorising the change, and actions arising**. ([David Barker 8.5.6](https://davidbarker.consulting/iso9001/clause-8-5-6-control-of-changes/)) | VO/SI files: who authorised, what was reviewed, what changed downstream |
| **8.7 Control of nonconforming outputs** | Identify nonconforming work, **segregate/contain/correct it**, re-verify after correction, and **retain records of the nonconformity, actions taken, concessions, and the authority deciding the disposition**. ([David Barker 8.7](https://davidbarker.consulting/iso9001/clause-8-7-control-of-nonconforming-outputs/)) | NCR log: defect → disposition → re-inspection → closure, with names |
| **9.1 Monitoring, measurement, analysis & evaluation** | Decide **what** to monitor, **when**, and **how**; **retain results as evidence**; analyse and evaluate (conformity, performance trends, supplier performance). ([iso9001help 9.1 via 8.1 records guidance](https://www.iso9001help.co.uk/8.1-Operational-Planning-and-Control.html), [Pretesh Biswas 8.1](https://preteshbiswas.com/2023/09/04/iso-90012015-clause-8-1-operational-planning-and-control/)) | Progress vs plan, inspection results, trend charts for management |
| **9.2 Internal audit** | Audit **programme** (frequency, methods, responsibility, reporting); objective auditors; results reported to management; **documented info retained as evidence of programme + results**. ([ISMS.online 9.2](https://www.isms.online/iso-9001/clause-9-2-internal-audit/), [David Barker 9.2](https://davidbarker.consulting/iso9001/clause-9-2-internal-audit/)) | Audit schedule, completed checklists, findings → corrective actions |
| **9.3 Management review** | Top management reviews the QMS at planned intervals; **inputs include audit results, nonconformities & corrective-action status, monitoring results, customer feedback**; outputs = decisions on improvement/resources; **retained as documented info**. ([iso-9001-checklist 9.3](https://www.iso-9001-checklist.co.uk/9.3-management-review.htm)) | Minutes + data pack of KPIs, NCR stats, audit findings |
| **10.2 Nonconformity & corrective action** | React, evaluate the need to eliminate the **root cause**, implement actions, **review effectiveness**, update risks; **retain documented info on the nature of nonconformities, actions taken, and results**. ([Auditor Training Online 10.2](https://blog.auditortrainingonline.com/blog/iso-9001-clause-10.2-nonconformity-and-corrective-action), [iso9001help 10.2](https://www.iso9001help.co.uk/10.2-Nonconformity-and-Corrective-Action.html)) | CAR register: root cause, action, verification of effectiveness, closure |

Also relevant but secondary: **7.1.5** (calibration of monitoring/measuring equipment), **7.2** (competence records), **8.4** (control of externally provided products — material receiving), **8.6** (release of products — final inspection before handover).

## 4. HK-specific notes — what government tenders actually ask, and how the app's audit trail strengthens a bid

### 4.1 ISO 9001 certification is a *list prerequisite*, not just nice-to-have
- DEVB: "to enhance quality management in public works construction, certain groups of consultants and contractors are required to obtain **quality management system certification**", with certificates accepted under the **Hong Kong Certification Body Accreditation Scheme (HKAS)** or equivalent — governed by **Works Bureau Technical Circular No. 13/2001** ([DEVB other information page](https://www.devb.gov.hk/en/construction_sector_matters/other_information/index.html), [WBTC 13/2001 PDF](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/158/1/wb1301.pdf), [Annex](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/69/1/C-2001-13-A-1.pdf)).
- The **Contractor Management Handbook** (admission/retention rules for the [List of Approved Contractors for Public Works](https://www.devb.gov.hk/en/construction_sector_matters/contractors/lists_of_public_works/index.html) and the [List of Approved Suppliers of Materials and Specialist Contractors](https://www.devb.gov.hk/en/construction_sector_matters/contractors/supplier/index.html)) embeds the ISO 9001 requirement in its minimum management criteria; it even specifies what to do when a contractor has no live contract to audit — a certification body accredited under HKCAS must confirm a full review of the **Quality Manual of the Hong Kong office** ([CMH page](https://www.devb.gov.hk/en/construction_sector_matters/contractors/contractor_management_handbook/index.html), [CMH Rev 27 (Apr 25) PDF](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/en/content_187/CMH_Rev27%20(Apr25).pdf)).
- **Housing Authority**: since **April 1993** all new-works building contractors tendering for HKHA projects must be ISO 9000-series certified ([HKHA housing conference paper](https://www.housingauthority.gov.hk/housconf/dhspeech.htm)); performance on HA sites is continuously scored under **PASS** ([Building PASS](https://www.housingauthority.gov.hk/en/business-partnerships/resources/performance-assessment/building-pass/index.html)).
- Individual works departments also write ISO 9001 QMS obligations directly into contracts (example: [WSD contract CE3614 ISO 9001 requirements](https://www.wsd.gov.hk/filemanager/en/content_190/CE3614_ISO9001.pdf)).
- Certification itself is bought from bodies like **HKQAA** ([construction certification services](https://www.hkqaa.org/en_certservice.php?catid=7)); the app's role is to make the certification + annual surveillance audits *pass cheaply* by having the records exist as a by-product of daily work.

### 4.2 Tender technical assessment scores what the app evidences
- Works tenders use marking schemes per **DEVB TC(W) No. 8/2004 "Tender Evaluation of Works Contracts"** and **TC(W) No. 4/2014**: technical attributes include **quality assurance / construction quality plans and past performance**, evaluated two-envelope before price is opened ([TC(W) 8/2004](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/77/1/C-2004-08-0-1_new.pdf), [TC(W) 4/2014](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/332/1/C-2014-04-04.pdf), [CEDD PAH 2024 Ch. 6](https://www.cedd.gov.hk/filemanager/eng/content_80/PAH%202024%20Chapter%206%20Rev%2000_Dec%202024.pdf)).
- **Past performance is fed by contractor performance reports under the CMH** — poor site-record keeping and unresolved disputes drag scores. The app's SI/VO/issue/daily evidence directly supports the contractor's side in performance-report representations and EOT/VO claims (CEDD [PAH 2024 Ch. 7 Contract Management](https://www.cedd.gov.hk/filemanager/eng/content_80/PAH%202024%20Chapter%207%20Rev%2000_Dec%202024.pdf) describes the engineer's site-diary/records regime the contractor's own records must stand against).
- The **site diary is contractual**: GCC for Civil Engineering Works Clause 32 (site diary & labour returns) — the app's `dailies` table with its same-day-only edit window is a credible contractor-side counterpart, but should grow labour/plant counts and photos to match what the Engineer's diary records.

### 4.3 The DWSS tailwind
- **DEVB TC(W) No. 8/2025** (superseding TC(W) No. 2/2023, originally TC(W) No. 3/2020) mandates a **Digital Works Supervision System** for Capital Works Programme contracts with pre-tender estimate **> $30M tendered on/after 1 Apr 2026**: digital **RISC forms, site diary/site record book with review-and-endorsement workflow, safety inspection records, cleansing inspection checklists, labour returns**, with linkage of inspection history and photo records ([TC(W) 8/2025](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/1404/1/C-2025-08-01.pdf), [TC(W) 3/2020](https://www.devb.gov.hk/filemanager/technicalcirculars/en/upload/374/1/C-2020-03-01.pdf), [HKICBIM summary](https://www.hkicbim.org/devb_tcw_no3-2020/)).
- Sales angle: main contractors on government jobs must run a DWSS; **subcontractors (判頭) are the weakest link feeding it**. The app gives a 判頭/小型總承建商 the same discipline at their scale, and its records (dailies ≈ site diary, future G2 ≈ RISC forms) map 1:1 onto what the main contractor's DWSS asks them for.

### 4.4 E-signature and e-record validity
- The **Electronic Transactions Ordinance (Cap. 553)** gives electronic records and signatures the same legal status as paper; they are prima facie admissible in HK courts ([Cap. 553](https://www.elegislation.gov.hk/hk/cap553), [Digital Policy Office ETO page](https://www.digitalpolicy.gov.hk/en/our_work/digital_infrastructure/legal_framework/regulation/eto/), [admissibility overview](https://www.esignglobal.com/blog/admissibility-e-signatures-hong-kong-courts)).
- Caveat to keep honest in sales copy: for transactions **with Government entities**, the ETO requires **digital signatures supported by recognised certificates** — so PTW signature-pad blobs (`permit_signoffs.signature_b64`, server-insert-only, `v10-ptw-schema.sql:124–128, 239–241`) are strong *internal QMS / inter-party* evidence, but statutory submissions to government still follow the relevant department's prescribed channel.

### 4.5 How to pitch the audit trail in a bid (one paragraph the sales kit can reuse)
> 本公司採用單一系統管理所有工地記錄：每項工地指令 (SI)、變更指令 (VO)、工作許可證 (PTW)、圖則版本、每日施工記錄及問題跟進均有不可篡改的時間戳、簽署人及審批鏈記錄，支援 ISO 9001:2015 第 7.5、8.5、9.1 條的文件化資訊要求，並可隨時匯出予審核員或工程師查閱。 — backed by: append-only `progress_history`/`approvals`, post-lock immutable SI/VO versions, immortal drawing blobs, same-day-locked dailies, and server-side PTW expiry.

### 4.6 What the app can *not* claim
- It does not make a company ISO 9001 certified — a quality manual/QMS scope, internal audits (G5), management reviews (G4) and a certification body audit are still required.
- It currently evidences **records (retain)** far better than **procedures (maintain)** — G3 (controlled documents) is what closes that half.
- Free-tier storage (1GB) vs. ISO/contractual retention expectations is a real audit-finding risk (G9).
