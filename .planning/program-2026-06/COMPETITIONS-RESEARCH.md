## 2. Detailed programme notes

### 2.1 HKICT Awards 2026 — the must-enter (deadline in ~5.5 weeks)

`[CONFIRMED 2026]` The 20th-anniversary HKICTA opened for enrolment **28 Apr 2026**; **deadline 20 Jul 2026 (12:00 noon)**; adjudication 17 Aug–25 Sep; ceremony **19 Nov 2026**. Enrolment is **free**. One Grand Award per category, plus a new **"Best Use of AI"** winner in each of the 8 categories.
Sources: [hkictawards.hk](https://www.hkictawards.hk/), [Bastille Post announcement](https://www.bastillepost.com/global/article/5812531-hkicta-2026-now-open-for-enrollment-celebrating-20-years-of-ict-excellence-in-hong-kong), [InvestHK listing](https://www.investhk.gov.hk/en/events/hong-kong-ict-awards-2026/), [Manila Times PR](https://www.manilatimes.net/2026/04/28/tmt-newswire/pr-newswire/hong-kong-ict-awards-2026-opens-for-enrolment/2330100).

**Category fit** (full list: [hkictawards.hk/award-categories](https://www.hkictawards.hk/award-categories/)). There is no construction category, so the realistic homes are:

1. **Smart Business Award → "Solution for SME" stream** (led by Hong Kong Computer Society) — best fit. CK工程 is literally an IT product "for effective use by SME" (判頭 firms ARE the HK SME archetype). Source: [hkcs.org.hk/sbictawards](https://hkcs.org.hk/sbictawards/).
2. **ICT Startup Award → "Digital Innovation Solution and Service"** (led by WTIA) — alternative if the startup framing is stronger. Verify WTIA-specific startup criteria (company age/size caps) on the WTIA entry page before choosing.

**Eligibility (Smart Business, confirmed):** HK-registered entity or HK resident; must hold IP rights; **product must have been in market / live operation ≥ 3 months** at submission. CK on iOS App Store since v1.x — confirm launch date gives ≥3 months before 20 Jul 2026 and keep App Store screenshots/版本歷史 as proof.

**Judging weights (Smart Business):** Innovation & Creativity 25% · Functionality 20% · Market Potential/Performance 20% · Benefits & Impact 20% · Quality 15%. ("Best Use of AI" judged separately: innovation 25% / impact 30% / UX 25% / ethics 20% — skip unless an AI feature ships before adjudication.)

**Prizes:** trophy + HKICTA logo usage rights + promotion + for qualifying SMEs **3 months free HK Science Park co-working** and **Cyberport Smart-Space Flexi desk** — useful real estate for a 1-founder company.

**How to be competitive:** judges in the SME stream reward *demonstrated deployment impact* over tech novelty. Lead with: real sites running it daily, quantified before/after (WhatsApp+paper → one system), the dispute-audit-trail story, and a 判頭 testimonial. The 20% "Market Potential" axis wants a credible expansion story (Android launch + CITF listing + GBA). Past HKICTA winners typically show: live customers, a demo that works in front of judges, and a clear "why only in HK" localisation moat (PTW 動火證 terminology, HKD VO chains).

**Note:** a single product is generally entered into ONE category — don't double-file Smart Business and ICT Startup without checking the cross-entry rule with the secretariat.

### 2.2 CITF — Construction Innovation and Technology Fund (the revenue lever)

`[CONFIRMED 2026]` Not a competition — but it is the single highest-leverage government programme for this app. CITF is **open for application year-round**, and effective **1 May 2026** each applicant has a combined cap of **HK$10M** with **SMEs getting a co-fund ratio 10% higher than standard**.
Sources: [citf.cic.hk](https://citf.cic.hk/), [CITF Guide to Application (PDF)](https://www.citf.cic.hk/Product_Photo/files/Framework%20and%20T&C/CITF%20Guide%20to%20Application_Eng.pdf), [CITF FAQ](https://www.citf.cic.hk/?route=faq), [Pre-approved Lists](https://www.citf.cic.hk/?route=search), [Audit Commission report](https://www.aud.gov.hk/pdf_e/e80ch03sum.pdf).

- **Who gets funded:** levy-paying contractors (CIC levy in preceding 24 months), **registered specialist trade contractors / subcontractors** (= CK's 判頭 customers), listed consultants; "other players… case-by-case". The founder's company is the *vendor*, not the applicant.
- **The play: get CK工程 onto the Pre-approved Technologies List** (584 products as of 15 Apr 2026). Once listed, any contractor/判頭 buying the app gets a **streamlined application** and co-funding — the government effectively discounts your product for your customers. Vetting requires past selling-price records and a cost-benefit assessment by the Vetting Sub-committee; approved pricing becomes public and acts as the funding ceiling.
- **Action:** contact the CITF Secretariat for the technology-listing submission route (the public site shows the list and applicant guide; the vendor-side listing form is obtained via the secretariat). Prepare: product spec, pricing history, deployment evidence, productivity/safety benefit quantification.
- **Caveat:** an Audit Commission review of CITF exists (above PDF) — expect tightened evidence standards on benefit claims; that favours an app with a built-in audit trail.

### 2.4 Cyberport CCMF — HK$100k seed grant

`[CONFIRMED schedule pattern; next intake date needs verify]` Up to **HK$100,000** over a 6-month programme; eligibility: **HKID holder (individual)** or **HK-incorporated limited company**; multiple intakes per year. The **Jun 2026 intake closed 1 Apr 2026**; the next intake is expected ~Q3/Q4 2026 (historically ~3 intakes/yr — Mar/Jun/Oct pattern).
Sources: [Cyberport CCMF page](https://www.cyberport.hk/en/entrepreneurship/hong_kong_programme/), [HKUST EC — Jun 2026 intake notice](https://ec.hkust.edu.hk/events/application-ccmf-and-cip-jun-2026-intake-cyberport-entrepreneurship-programme-information), [FundFluent summary](https://www.fundfluent.io/funding-programs/hk-cyberport-creative-micro-fund), [SME Link](https://www.smelink.gov.hk/en/web/sme-portal/w/cyberport-creative-micro-fund.html).
**Fit note:** CCMF normally funds *projects at concept/prototype stage* — an already-shipped App Store product may read as "too late" for CCMF but **right on time for the Cyberport Incubation Programme (CIP, HK$500k over 24 months)**, which accepts companies with a working product < 7 years old. Apply CIP, not CCMF, if BR/incorporation < 7 yrs — same intake calendar. Smart construction / proptech is inside Cyberport's "digital tech" remit.
**Judges weight:** innovation, business viability, social impact, team execution. A live product with paying-ready users is a strong CIP application; the weak point to pre-empt is "1-founder team" — show advisor bench or hiring plan.

### 4.1 Materials needed (build once, reuse everywhere)

1. **10-slide deck** (zh-HK master + EN variant): problem (WhatsApp+paper+memory), product walkthrough, the audit-trail story, traction metrics, roadmap (Android, AI feature, GBA), team/advisors.
2. **90-second demo video** — the Asia Smart Innovation entry format (1-min, EN subtitles) is the binding constraint; cut a 60s version from it. Script the dispute scenario: instruction issued → progress ticked → issue raised → escalated → PTW signed via QR — all timestamped.
3. **Metrics pack** (query the live Supabase): # projects/sites, # active users by role, # progress updates, # issues raised/resolved + median resolution time, # PTW sign-offs, # SI/VO through the approval chain. Judges in the SME stream want deployment numbers, not feature lists.
4. **Security & compliance one-pager:** RLS on every table with two formal hardening passes (`supabase/v17-user-profiles-rls-hardening.sql`, `supabase/v18-rls-audit-hardening.sql`); Apple-compliant account deletion with FK cascade (`supabase/v6-account-deletion.sql`, `supabase/v20-delete-account-fk-cascade.sql`); immutable progress history (`supabase/v3-5-progress-extras.sql:19` creates `progress_history`; `supabase/v25-progress-snapshots.sql` adds point-in-time snapshots); QR permit verification with per-scan audit rows (`supabase/v10-ptw-schema.sql:14` — `permit_scans` table, line 124 — `permit_signoffs` with signature blobs).
5. **Reference customer:** one named 判頭 or 工地主任 willing to give a 2-line quote and (ideally) speak to judges. The Smart Business interview round will ask for this.
6. **BR/incorporation documents** — HKICTA needs proof of registration; Cyberport CIP needs a HK limited company; CITF vendor listing needs pricing records.

### 4.2 The pitch angle — how to frame CK工程 for HK judges

- **Lead: "the audit trail that survives disputes."** Every instruction, permit, drawing, progress tick and issue is captured with who/when, immutable history and snapshots (see §4.1.4). In an industry where payment disputes are settled by whoever kept better records, this is a quantifiable benefit — frame it as risk/claims cost avoided, not as a feature.
- **Second: ISO 9001 enablement for small contractors.** ISO 9001 demands documented control of records, traceability and corrective-action workflows — exactly what `progress_history`, the issue escalation chain (`src/contexts/IssuesContext.tsx`, handler chain in `src/types.ts`) and the SI/VO approval RPC (`supabase/v9-rpc-submit-approval.sql`) produce as a by-product of daily use. Pitch: "a 判頭 using CK工程 daily is already generating ISO 9001-grade records" — this resonates with judges scoring "Benefits & Impact" and with CITF's productivity-evidence requirement.
- **Third: real 判頭 adoption, zh-HK native.** No competing product speaks HK site language: PTW types use HK industry terminology (動火證 fire-watch staffing in `supabase/v32-ptw-fire-watch.sql`, `v37-ptw-safety-officer-staffing.sql`), VO quotations in HKD, eight site roles with per-project RLS rights (`supabase/v27-progress-rights-by-membership.sql`). Localisation depth is the moat — say so explicitly; HK judges reward solutions built *for* HK rather than ported.
- **Defuse the weaknesses before judges find them:** 1-founder (answer: advisor bench + Cyberport/HKSTP programme support + hiring milestone); no AI (answer: roadmap item with a concrete first feature, and note the architecture — single Supabase backend — makes an AI layer additive); Android pending (answer: build verified on BlueStacks, Play identity verification in progress — show the timeline).
