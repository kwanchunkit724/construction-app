## Slide order rationale

1-3: Empathy. Don't pitch yet.
4-5: Tell them what you do.
6: SHOW (this is the magic — never skip).
7: Why not competitors.
8: Trust (case study).
9-10: Money + security (justify price).
11: Future roadmap (reduce switching risk).
12: Easy close.

## Tips

- Slide 6 (demo) is 80% of your sale. **Practice it 10 times before any client meeting.**
- Don't put logos of T3/T4 clients you haven't actually closed. Lying loses trust faster than no logos.
- If asked "who else uses it" and you don't have public refs yet: "We just launched on App Store May 27. You'd be one of the first 10 paying customers — special pricing protects your slot." This works as scarcity + honest positioning.
- Have a backup plan if internet fails mid-demo: pre-record 60-sec video on phone, play locally if Wi-Fi dies.
- Bring physical handout: 1-page A4 PDF version of slides for them to take away.
