# 14 — Social Media & Customer Acquisition Plan

How to use social media + content to attract HK construction customers, the
**booking → 1-month-free** offer mechanics, and the broader acquisition
playbook. Honest framing first so you don't waste money/time.

---

## 0. Honest framing (read first)

- **Social media is NOT your lead engine.** 45–60yo 老總 don't buy site
  software off IG/TikTok. Warm outreach + referral + demo (the GTM plan) is
  the engine. **Social = a credibility layer.** When you DM a prospect, they
  google you / check your FB / LinkedIn before replying. A real presence with
  demo content makes them reply and trust you.
- **Real app footage > AI video for B2B trust.** Your free 60-sec Loom (real
  app) is your best asset. AI-generated video is for *hooks* — a 6-sec scroll-
  stopper, site b-roll, a logo sting — not for faking the product.
- **Spend order:** free first (Loom, organic posts, Canva). Pay for AI
  generation / ads only after organic + outreach are running and you've seen
  what resonates.

## 3. Content pillars (what to post) + 2-week starter

5 pillars, rotate them. Each post ends with a soft CTA + the /sell link.

1. **Pain mirror** — a relatable site problem ("揾唔到上個月邊個簽嗰張 PTW…").
2. **Show the app** — 15-30s clip of one feature (daily / 急件 / 4-zone).
3. **Proof / trust** — "App Store 已上架", security, the 關春傑 pilot numbers.
4. **Education** — "點樣 30 秒交 daily report"; HK 工程 tips.
5. **Offer** — the 1-month-free pilot, founding price, "book a demo".

**Starter calendar (3 posts/week × 2 weeks):**
| Day | Pillar | Post |
|---|---|---|
| W1 Mon | Pain | WhatsApp/紙簿 chaos photo + 1 line + /sell |
| W1 Wed | Show app | the 60s Loom (or a 20s cut) |

| W2 Mon | Proof | App Store badge + "25/25 security blocked" |
| W2 Wed | Education | "30 秒交 daily" short clip |
| W2 Fri | Pain→Offer | dispute story + founding price 6/30 |

Post native (don't just paste links — platforms throttle link posts; put the
link in first comment or the bio CTA).

## 5. The booking → 1-month-free offer (the conversion mechanism)

The pilot is **already HK$0 for 1 month** — package it as an *offer* with a
*booking* hook + *urgency*.

**Offer:** 「預約 demo / 留低聯絡 → 即享 1 個月免費試用（我幫你 set 好一個 zone）」
plus the founding lock: 「6 月 30 號前簽 = HK$2,850/月 鎖一年」.

**What counts as a "booking":** a submitted `/#/sell` form OR a booked demo
slot. Both land in **/#/mission → 潛在客戶** so you can work the pipeline
(new → contacted → demo → pilot → won).

**Mechanics (already built + one tweak):**
- `/#/sell` lead form captures the booking → leads table → mission Leads tab.
- Every social CTA points to `/#/sell`.
- (tweak shipping now) a clear "限時：預約即送 1 個月免費" banner on /sell.

**Rules to protect it:**
- Free month requires a real project + a 30-min onboarding (you set it up).
- One free month per company. Continues at Standard unless cancelled in writing
  7 days before period end (it's in 06-PRICING agreement).
- Urgency: founding price ends 6/30 → real deadline, use it.

## 6. How to attract MORE customers (the full playbook, ranked by ROI)

1. **Warm network + referrals (highest ROI).** Your contacts + 關春傑 上下游.
   After the first happy pilot, ask for 1–2 intros (Script 6). Offer a referral
   reward (HK$2,000 or 1 free month — in 06-PRICING).
2. **Direct outreach** (LinkedIn/WhatsApp) per `10-OUTREACH-DRAFTS` + Loom.
   This is the engine. Social just makes it convert better.
3. **Subcontractor (判頭) channel.** Happy 判頭 push the 老總 to adopt (they love
   the 物料 + 急件 alert). Sell them on that, they sell up.
4. **Industry bodies + events.** CIC / HKCA / RICS member directories +
   seminars (02-MARKET-CHANNELS). Face-to-face → business card → WhatsApp + Loom.
5. **Developer / T4 channel (biggest multiplier, slow).** One property developer
   project director who mandates the app across their GCs = 10× customers from
   one relationship. Long game; plant it early.
6. **Social/content (this doc)** — credibility + inbound trickle; supports 1–4.
7. **Paid ads** — only AFTER organic + outreach prove the message. FB lead ads
   targeting HK construction/contractor interests can work, but spend small,
   measure cost-per-booking in /#/mission, kill if > a demo's worth of your time.

**Track everything in `/#/mission`** (outreach_sent, replies, demos, pilots,
customers) — same funnel as `11-GTM-TESTING-PLAN`.

## 7. Realistic expectations + first moves

- Social will NOT flood you with leads. It makes outreach + referrals convert.
- **First 3 moves (this week):** ① set up LinkedIn + FB + YouTube (host Loom),
  ② post the Loom + 1 pain post + 1 offer post, ③ keep sending warm outreach
  with the /sell link. Booking comes from outreach; social closes the trust gap.
- Revisit paid ads / AI video spend once you have 1 paying customer + a message
  you know resonates.
