## DO NOT SAY (lessons from B2B sales)

- ❌ "Revolutionary"  / "AI-powered" — buzzwords scare off senior 老總
- ❌ "Save 50%" — too vague, can't substantiate
- ❌ "World's first" — they'll Google and find Procore
- ❌ "It's free" without context — they'll think you're a student project
- ❌ Long product feature lists — they buy outcomes not features

## DO SAY

- ✅ Specific pain quote: "Foreman 每日 daily report 攞到唔齊"
- ✅ Specific timestamp: "我可以 send 你 1 分鐘 video"
- ✅ Specific outcome: "Office real-time 睇晒 4 個 zone 進度"
- ✅ Easy ask: "5 分鐘 demo OK?"
- ✅ Easy out: "唔啱用就當無事傾過"
