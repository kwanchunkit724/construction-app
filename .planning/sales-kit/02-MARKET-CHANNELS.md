# 02 — Where to Find Clients

## Free Channels (start here)

### 1. LinkedIn — highest ROI for B2B HK construction
- Search: "工地", "site supervisor", "project manager construction Hong Kong", "main contractor HK"
- Filter: HK-located, 50-500 employees, construction/civil/architecture industry
- Daily action: 20 connection requests with personalized note. 5 will accept. 1-2 will DM back.
- Personalized note template (zh-HK, 200 chars):
  > 你好 [Name]，做緊construction industry tech，CK工程剛喺 App Store 上架幫小型 GC 取代 WhatsApp+紙簿。睇咗你嘅 background，想請教 1-2 個關於 PTW + 日誌嘅 pain point。可以連線傾下？

### 2. Hong Kong Construction Industry WhatsApp / Signal groups
- Active groups exist for: PTW signoff officers, RICS HK members, BCA young professionals.
- Don't spam. Lurk first 2 weeks. When someone posts a relevant problem, DM 1-on-1.
- Find through CIC events — exchange WhatsApp at networking events.

### 3. Construction Industry Council (CIC) events
- Monthly seminars at CIC Headquarters, Kowloon Bay.
- Cost: Free for individuals, HK$200-500 for non-members.
- Schedule: https://www.cic.hk/eng/main/events_seminars/
- Bring: 50 business cards + iPad with app preview running.
- Target: 老總 / PM attending sessions on "Construction 4.0" / "Digital Transformation".

### 4. HK Trade Development Council (HKTDC) Construction Expo
- Annual event (usually March), HKCEC.
- Exhibitor option: HK$15,000+ for small booth. Skip for v1.
- Visitor option: free badge → walk booths → talk to GC representatives 1-on-1.
- Target: subcontractor booths showing tools/equipment — talk to founder, they're your buyer.

### 5. BCA (HK Construction Association) member directory
- https://www.hkca.com.hk/membership/list-of-members/
- Lists 200+ member firms. Each has contact person + email.
- Action: pull list to spreadsheet. Filter by member size. Email/LinkedIn top 50 personally.

### 6. RICS HK chapter (Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors)
- 5,000+ QSurveyors + PMs in HK. Run CPD events.
- Become a guest speaker on "Digital Tools for HK Construction" — instant credibility + 100+ contacts in one event.
- Contact: hongkong@rics.org

### 7. HKIE (HK Institution of Engineers) Civil + Building Division
- Similar to RICS but for engineers. Sponsored events at HK Engineers Centre, Wan Chai.
- Same play — speak at a meetup.

## Paid Channels (after you have 3 paying customers)

### 8. Google Ads / Meta Ads
- Target keywords: 「地盤管理 app」「日誌系統」「PTW 簽核」「工地軟件」
- Budget: HK$3,000-5,000/month to start.
- Land on dedicated landing page (you have lime-six prod — make a /sell page or buy `ck-construction.app` domain).
- Convert rate: expect 2-5% lead form fill, 10-20% of those become demos.

### 9. WhatsApp Business broadcast
- Build list via networking + LinkedIn DMs.
- Weekly: 1 short message about new feature OR case study OR construction industry tip.
- Don't spam — value-first, sales at end.

### 10. Construction trade magazines (HK)
- Construction Hong Kong magazine, 建造科技 magazine.
- Display ad: HK$5,000-15,000 per issue. Skip — low ROI.
- Editorial article: try to pitch a guest article on "如何用 mobile app 取代地盤 WhatsApp" — free credibility.

## Referral Channels (your goldmine)

### 11. Existing customer referrals
- After 1 month of pilot success → ask 老總: "Who else in your network is suffering same WhatsApp chaos?"
- Offer: HK$2,000 referral credit OR 1 month free for each successful referral that signs up.
- Track in a spreadsheet.

### 12. Trade associations as channel partners
- HK General Building Contractors Association — propose member discount (10% off for HKGCA members).
- They promote to their 100+ members for you.

### 13. Existing software partners
- Cubicost / Glodon HK distributor — they sell QS software, NOT daily ops. Refer leads to each other.
- Reach out via LinkedIn to their HK sales lead.

## Networks You Have Already

You said 12 alpha testers. **Each tester knows 5-10 other 老總 / PM**. Easiest path:
- WhatsApp each tester individually after 1 week of v1.1: "點呀，用得順未？" → ask them for 1 intro to another 老總 friend.
- Don't ask "who would buy this" — ask "who else suffers the same daily report pain?"

## Where NOT to Waste Time (in first 6 months)

- Government tender boards — too long, too formal.
- Cold-emailing large GC procurement teams — gatekept, ignored.
- Posting on Facebook construction groups — spammy, low quality leads.
- Reddit r/construction — US-dominated.
- 中國大陸 (mainland China) markets — different regulatory + market.
